ANIMATION
ELASTIC COLLISION
m1
m2
Here,
               m1=m2
KEY POINTS :
1> The Momentum is conserved.
2> Total Energy is conserved.
3> The Kinetic Energy is conserved.
4> Forces involved during the collision are conservative.
INELASTIC COLLISION
m1
m2
Here,
               m1 is less than m2
KEY POINTS :
1> The Momentum is conserved.
2> Total Energy is not conserved.
3> The Kinetic Energy is not conserved.
4> Sum or all the forces involved are not conservative.
